Tell your story.

What is a Memento? It’s a simple, yet beautiful way to present a story. Whether a family history, a life story, or an individual event, Memento allows you to create a stunning visual online presentation. Assemble your story, photos, video and sound elements into a seamless, scrollable presentation that you can make public or share with a select audience.

Your Memento presentation is stored in the cloud along with all the elements that you’ve assembled to make it, so you can access it anywhere and even show it on mobile devices. Want to get started? Click here to create your own.

create a memento

How does it work?

Creating a Memento is all about gathering your cherished memories in their various forms… Stories. Photos. Videos. Music.

Once you’ve collected these elements, you can organize them and use them to embellish your story, placing them on a timeline. Then, pick the photos you’d like to emphasize, and make them backgrounds. Include smaller images throughout your story. Add music and film clips if you like. Once all your elements are in place, preview your Memento presentation, and save it for viewing and sharing.

STEP ONE
Gather your elements

When we think of memories, photographs often come to mind. And Memento.me is a great place to gather, store and organize them into a Memento presentation. But don’t forget to think about including videos, music or other sound clips. Add a voice recording that retells a part of the story. Or why not add a graphic of a family tree, or a map? All these make the story come alive. Files can be easily uploaded and organized on Memento.me to create your Memento presentation.

STEP TWO
Curate your Memento

Use one of our templates to curate the elements of your Memento. Drop in your story and your collected elements and use one of our templates to help sequence elements, place scrolling text over an image or video, and place images and video as smaller elements within your story.

Or you can create your own custom Memento presentation using the timeline which offers flexibility for a truly unique presentation. The timeline allows you to place the elements as you’d like, preview the presentation and rearrange as desired.

STEP THREE
Save your Memento

When your Memento is ready, save it and feel free to make it public. You can allow full or limited access as you like, or keep it completely private. Your files are saved in the cloud and always accessible should you want to add to or re-curate your Memento.
